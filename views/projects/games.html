The Channeler

Unreal Engine 4, using the Tobii EyeX hardware for eye tracking

Team of 13 (4 producers, 5 programmers, 4 artists)

(December 15, 2015 - Present)

Utilizing eye-tracking technology, the development team hopes to innovate with new and exciting mechanics that take advantage of human reaction speed and eye reflexes. We are currently using the Tobii EyeX to track the players eye gaze.
The Channeler takes place in a world populated by ghosts and ghouls unseen by the regular eye; but you are a Channeler, blessed with the Third Eye and able to commune with these spirits.
Mega spirits which haven't appeared in centuries have begun to pop up around the world; it is up to you to send them back to the spirit world.

Dystopian Drill Divers

Unity 2D, C#, Android

Team of 5 (2 Producers, 2 Programmers, 1 Artist)

(November 3, 2015 - November 17, 2015)

A 2 week rapid prototype of an Android game developed in Unity Engine, FIEA (Fall 2015)

Guide your robot around obstacles as it drills down to the earth's core, in a hope to extract its heat as the last remaining source of fuel for mankind. The farther down you drill, the harder the earth's crust. The faster you drill, the more quickly your robot's fuel burns up. Maintain a steady balance between drilling speed and fuel consumption rate.

Obstacles inlclude rocks and diamond mines.
On your way down don't forget to collect fuel for your robot, and diamonds for buying upgrades from the store.

Special power upgradeds include a "phaser laser", which will destroy everything in it's path, and a "radioactive core heater" which burns out the debris around to make way for the robot.

Kite Fite

Adobe Flash, ActionScript 3.0

Team of 5 (1 producer, 2 programmers, 2 artists)

(Sept. 1, 2015 - Sept. 15, 2015)
Play the demo here
Use the Weapon Creation Tool
Check out the code for KiteFite (including the weapon creation tool)

Kite Fite is a 2 player kite fighting game developed in Adobe Flash ActionScript 3.0 as part of a 2 week Rapid Prototyping project at FIEA. The objective of the game is to knock out the opponents kite using a weapon attached to the tail of your kite. Players control using WASD and arrow keys respectively.

The programmers also designed a weapon creation tool for the producer to experiment with the different attributes of the weapons like speed, damage amount and flexibility (length and number of links of the rope through which it is attached to the kite).

Note that the polygon must be convex and the vertices must be drawn in clockwise order. The tool was meant for producer use only and thus complex validations for determining whether the polygon is convex or the vertices are in clockwise order have not been implemented.

Pacman : The Elements

Adobe Flash, ActionScript 3.0

Project type: Individual

(October 2013)
Play the demo here

Inspired by the legendary Pacman from the DOS age, this game took the theme of "The Elements" and incorporated them as special collectable powers -

Earth - Build a horizontal or vertical wall on a single tile to block the path of the monsters
Air - Click on a tile to teleport to that spot
Fire - The mosters will burn on touching you... Enjoy the invincibility for 10 seconds
Water - The floor turns to ice and allows you to slide on it for a quick getaway... Be careful though, the ice melts in 10 seconds
Ether - You become a "heavenly body" as monsters cannot harm you for 10 seconds

The game has unlimited lives. The aim is to score as many points as possible and reach the highest level possible. Every time you are caught by the mosters, 50 points are deducted. Once you cross a level, the number of seconds you took to complete the level is subtracted from you total.
